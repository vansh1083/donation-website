Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2025

Global Relief Fund (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a registered MSME entity (UDYAM-JK-19-0015271). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and transparent manner.

1. Information We Collect

To process your donations and maintain transparency, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Details: Name, Email Address, and Phone Number.
  • Transaction Data: Donation amount, currency, and date of transaction.

2. Payment Information Security

Your financial security is our top priority. We DO NOT store, process, or retain any card numbers, UPI IDs, PINs, or bank account details. All payment transactions are securely handled by certified third-party payment gateways like Razorpay and Slice UPI.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Processing Donations: To complete the transactions you initiate.
  • Communication: To send donation receipts and impact updates via email or phone.
  • Legal Compliance: To maintain financial records for tax and audit purposes as per Indian Law.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain donation records (Name, Email, Amount) for as long as necessary to comply with legal and tax obligations (typically 7 years). After this period, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. For any such requests, please contact our grievance officer at the email provided below.
