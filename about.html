Our Mission: Immediate Impact, Lasting Change

Global Relief Fund was founded on the principle that aid should be fast, transparent, and directly accessible to those who need it most. Our mission is to mobilize resources quickly in times of crisis—whether it is a natural disaster, medical emergency, or environmental restoration.

Our Core Focus Areas

  • Disaster Response: Providing immediate food, shelter, and rebuilding support after natural calamities like floods and earthquakes.
  • Medical Emergency Aid: Funding life-saving treatments for families who cannot afford essential medical surgery bills.
  • Family & Incident Support: Offering financial aid to victims and families affected by sudden crises or accidents.
  • Tree Plantation Drives: Restoring habitats and combating climate change through active community plantation projects.
